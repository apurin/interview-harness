The interface gives each decision more breathing room and emphasizes rationale, making it friendlier for first-time or high-stakes review.

Clarity Each step has one job, reducing accidental decisions.
Guidance The user sees why a choice matters before acting.
Tradeoff Repeat users may find the flow slower than a dense workspace.
